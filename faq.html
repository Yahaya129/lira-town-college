Frequently Asked Questions

1. What courses does Lira Town College offer?
We offer a full secondary school curriculum including Sciences, Arts, and Vocational subjects in both O-Level and A-Level.
2. Where is the school located?
Lira Town College is located along Obote Avenue in Lira City, Northern Uganda.
3. How do I apply for admission?
Admission forms can be picked from the school office or downloaded from our website during the intake period.
4. Does the school provide boarding facilities?
Yes, we offer both boys' and girls' boarding facilities with supervision and support for student welfare.
5. How can I contact the school?
You can reach us via phone at +256 772 123456 or email us at info@liratowncollege.ac.ug.
